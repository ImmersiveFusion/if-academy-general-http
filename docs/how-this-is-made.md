# How this is made

*Last reviewed by Immersive Fusion on 2026-06-03.*

This publication is written with AI assistance. We are telling you that here, on its own page, because it would be strange not to. We publish essays about living well alongside AI. It would be a peculiar kind of dishonesty to make them without AI and pretend otherwise, or to make them with AI and hide it.

So here is the arrangement, plainly.

## What the machine does, and what a person does

An AI helps draft and shape this writing. A person reads every line, decides what stays and what gets cut, checks the claims, and is the one who says a piece is ready. The machine produces; the person decides. When a sentence is wrong, it is wrong because a person let it through, and the correction is a person's to make.

That is the same arrangement the [essays themselves](essays/we-will-be-ok/index.md) are about. The argument we keep making is that a good AI surfaces what it knows and stops at the line where the call becomes a human's, and that the human has to stay the one who decides. We hold ourselves to it here. The name on this publication is *Immersive Fusion*, and the name is there because the deciding was ours.

## Why we don't put a "written by humans" badge on it

You may have noticed those lately, at the top of long articles: a small note insisting no AI was involved, meant to reassure you that what follows is not slop. We understand the worry. We are answering it the other way.

We will not tell you a machine did not touch this, because one did. The reassurance we can honestly offer is not *no machine was here.* It is *a person stands behind every word, and you can see whose name it is.* If a piece here reads as careless, that is a human failing of ours to fix, not the machine's to blame. The name at the bottom of the page is how you hold us to that.

## How mistakes are handled

The same way as everything else we publish, described in our [editorial commitments](commitments.md): when we get something wrong, we say so on the same page, dated, and we do not delete the original. An error that came from the AI and an error that came from a person are corrected identically, because to you they are the same error, and the responsibility for both is ours. If you spot one, you can write to us at [academy@immersivefusion.com](mailto:academy@immersivefusion.com), and we will answer.

## What this is not

This page is not a claim that AI makes the writing better, or that you should be impressed by it. The tool is a tool. Whether the writing is any good is a separate question, and it is yours to judge by reading it. This page is only here so that you know how it was made and who is accountable for it.
